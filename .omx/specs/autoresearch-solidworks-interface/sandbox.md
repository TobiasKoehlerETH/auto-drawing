## Sandbox

- Workspace root: `C:\Code\auto-drawing`
- OS: Windows
- SolidWorks is installed locally and a SolidWorks process is available.
- Live COM automation is allowed for local testing.
- No destructive system changes are required.

## Constraints

- Prefer direct evidence from builds, tests, and local runs.
- Do not overstate untested drawing capabilities.
- Keep recommendations focused on an agent-to-SolidWorks drawing pipeline.
- Treat `guidelines/` as the authoritative drawing standard for the future interface.
